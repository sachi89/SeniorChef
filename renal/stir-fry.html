Stir-Fry Meal

Ingredients:

 2 tablespoon cooking oil
2 medium chicken breast, cut in bite size pieces
1 10-ounce package frozen stir fry vegetables
​½ tablespoon low sodium soy sauce
2 cups cooked rice


Directions:

Heat oil in 9-10" skillet on high.

Add chicken, and sauté.

Stir in vegetables. Add soy sauce and stir well.

Reduce heat to medium high and cook uncovered for 3-5 minutes, or until done, stirring frequently.

Serve over ⅔ cup rice.
