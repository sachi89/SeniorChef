Low Sodium Spaghetti Sauce

Ingredients:

 1/4 cup chopped onions
15 oz no salt added tomato sauce (canned)
8 tablespoons of no salt added tomato past (Hunt’s)
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
3 tsps garlic, diced or minced
1 1/2 tsps ground oregano
2 tbsps leaves basil (dried)
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1 1/2 cups of water


Directions:

Spray a pan with cooking spray. Cook the onions until they’re translucent and add the tomato sauce and tomato paste; then water. Stir in all the spices, and let the sauce simmer on low for 30 minutes.

Optional: Add in ground beef or turkey. Use sodium free chicken broth in place of water.
