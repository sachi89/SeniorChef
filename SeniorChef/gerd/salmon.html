Grilled Salmon with Honey

Ingredients:

 240 g salmon fillet
2 tbsp honey, liquid
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp olive oil
1 pinch salt (optional)
ground pepper to taste (optional)


Directions:

Cut the salmon fillet into similarly sized pieces, as per the number of servings required, then put them in a resealable plastic bag. Add the honey and soy sauce, then mix well. Let marinate 2 hours in the refrigerator.

Place the fish on an oiled, hot grill, then cook 8 to 10 min, depending on thickness of the fillet pieces, turning them once. Alternatively, heat the oil in a grooved thick-bottom pan. When hot, add the fish, then cook 4-5 min per side, turning the pieces once. Check with a fork to see if the fish is cooked through.

Season with salt and pepper, then serve on the warmed plates.
