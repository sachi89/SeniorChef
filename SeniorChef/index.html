Welcome to SeniorChef!

The mission of SeniorChef is to help seniors with dietary restrictions due to chronic disorders. We do this by providing easy access to recipes that follow these restrictions with senior friendly features that make navigating through our website a breeze.

To begin, hover over Recipes on the menu bar at the top of the page and a dropdown menu should appear. Select your dietary restriction to discover some great new recipes you are sure to enjoy. If you have a recipe that you would like to share with our community, please feel free to submit your recipe by clicking on Submit a Recipe at the top menu. If you would like to recieve weekly newsletters and updates on new recipes, subscribe to our mailing list by clicking on Subscribe. If you have any questions, click on Contact Us to send us a message.
