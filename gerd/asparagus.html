Roasted Asparagus

Ingredients:

 20 asparagus, average size
4 tsp olive oil
1 pinch salt (optional)


Directions:

Preheat the oven to 230ºC/450ºF.

Clean the asparagus, then lay them on an oiled baking tray. Coat thoroughly with oil, then season with salt.

Cook in the middle of the oven until the asparagus change colour, about 8-10 min, depending on their size. Alternatively, asparagus may be wrapped in aluminum foil and cooked on an outdoor grill at 'medium-high'. For small quantities, asparagus may also be sautéed in a pan on the stovetop.
