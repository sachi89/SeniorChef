Beet and Peach BBQ Sauce (Tomato Free)

Ingredients:

 1 small beet
2 tbsp nitrate free molasses
1 tsp honey
1 ripe peach
pinch of salt
1 1/2 tbsp cider vinegar


Directions:

Place all ingredients in a high-speed blender and let it mix for 5 min.
